KNN Clustering Analysis of Exercise Habits

This is what our KNN analysis of peole's transportation habits concluded. You can divide our data on walking and driving into three categories. One category is composed of people who are very environmentally conscious, the green labelled dots. The next category is composed of people who are on the way to being environmentally conscious but still have some way to go, the orange dots. Finally, the red category is composed of people who are not environmentally conscious because they drive too much. We used Google Cloud's Machine Learning services to perform our calculations.
